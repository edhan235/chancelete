Our Mission

Student-athletes with a two-way commitment to excellence

Chanceletes.org empowers resource-constrained student-athletes who are willing to work hard, sacrifice and trust the process by investing in the wraparound support needed to reach their fullest potential.

In sport and in life.

The Problem We Solve

Most athletes devote close to 0% on "The Edge" activities during the critical phase of development. We change that by providing comprehensive support in nutrition, mental training, recovery, and academic excellence.

Who We Serve

01

First-Gen / Low-Income

Students from families earning below median income who will be first-generation college students. We believe in you, and we're here to level the playing field.

02

Physically Gifted

Athletes with demonstrable elite physical potential in their sport. Coaches see it. Scouts see it. You feel it. Now let's develop it.

03

Relentlessly Hungry

Athletes who understand that talent without discipline is potential wasted. You're willing to do whatever it takes—every single day.

Your Commitment

Show Up

Attend all training sessions, meetings, and check-ins. Excellence requires consistency. Missing sessions without valid reasons = removal from the program.

Work Hard

Give maximum effort in everything—training, academics, recovery. We can't make you better if you don't put in the work. Half-hearted effort gets you nowhere.

Be Coachable

Listen to feedback. Implement corrections. Trust the process even when it's uncomfortable. Your coaches have walked this path—respect their experience.

Give Back

When you succeed, help the next generation. Mentor younger athletes. Share your story. Build the community that lifted you up.

We invest everything in you. You invest everything in yourself.

That's the deal. No shortcuts. No excuses. Just results.

What We Provide

Elite Coaching

Work with experienced coaches who've trained Olympic and professional athletes. Technical refinement, tactical development, and competitive strategy.

Sports Nutrition

Customized meal plans, supplements, and education from registered sports dietitians. Fuel your body like a professional athlete.

Recovery & Therapy

Access to physical therapy, massage, cryotherapy, and other recovery modalities. Stay healthy and perform at your peak.

Academic Support

Tutoring, college prep, and time management coaching. You're a student first—we'll help you excel in the classroom.

Mental Training

Sports psychology, visualization techniques, and mental resilience training. Champions are built from the mind up.

Competition Funding

Travel, registration fees, and equipment for key competitions. Get exposure to college scouts and recruiters.

Equipment & Gear

Quality training equipment, competition gear, and technology to track your progress. No athlete left behind due to lack of equipment.

Mentorship Network

Connect with former student-athletes, professionals, and mentors who've walked your path and achieved their dreams.

Recruiting Support

Highlight reels, recruiting profiles, and direct connections to college coaches. We'll help you get noticed and recruited.

This isn't charity. This is investment in talent that deserves a fair shot.

Every resource a Division I scholarship athlete has access to—you'll have too.
