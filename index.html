Our Mission

To develop student-athletes with a two-way commitment to excellence

Chanceletes.org empowers resource-constrained student-athletes willing to outwork others, sacrifice and build positive lifelong habits by investing in the wraparound support needed to reach their fullest potential.

In sport and in life.

The Problem We Solve

Copy the Pros

Most athletes devote close to 0% on "The Edge" activities during middle and high school years, critical years for elite development. We change that by providing comprehensive resources for sleep, nutrition, access to fitness training & recovery, expenses associated with competing and recruiting, mental training & mentorship, injury prevention & rehab and academic support.

We Look For

01

Resource Limited Underdogs

Student-athletes from families with limited resources that simply want a level playing field to prove your potential. We love underdog, first-generation college student-athletes.

02

Naturally Driven and Gifted

Student-athletes with demonstrable elite physical, mental and emotional potential in their sport at a young age. Coaches see it. Scouts see it. You feel it. Now let's develop it.

03

Relentlessly Hunger for Greatness

Athletes who understand that talent without discipline is potential wasted and have something to prove. You're willing to do whatever it takes to be great, every single day.

Your Commitment

Be Honorable

Complete like a warrior but never compromise on your sportsmanship, integrity and respect for others. Honor the code of conduct of champions.

Be Coachable

Embrace and implement feedback. Trust the process over results even when it's uncomfortable. Respect coaches and staff and work towards long-term dreams.

Work Harder

Give maximum effort in your training, academics and recovery. Never go through the motions and stay hungrier than the competition.

Give Back

When you succeed, help the next generation. Mentor younger athletes. Share your story. Build the community that lifted you up.

We promise to invest in you if you invest everything in yourself.

Reject shortcuts. Reject excuses. Commit to the process.

What We Provide

Elite Coaching

Memberships to fitness center and access to coaches that can develop strength & conditioning programs and technical/tactical development specific to your sport.

Sports Nutrition

Customized meal plans, supplements and education from registered sports nutritionists. Fuel your body like a professional athlete to maximize your training and recovery.

Recovery & Therapy

Access to physical therapy, massage, cryotherapy, and other recovery modalities. Stay healthy and perform at your peak.

Equipment & Gear

Mentorship Network

Recruiting Support
