The Mission

Every year, thousands of exceptionally talented young athletes never reach their potential—not because they lack ability or drive, but because they lack resources.

Chancelete exists to change that equation. We provide comprehensive support—coaching, nutrition, recovery, academic resources, competition funding—to first-generation and low-income student-athletes who possess rare physical gifts and uncommon hunger.

We're not looking for athletes who want it. We're looking for athletes who need it and are willing to sacrifice everything to get it.

Excellence through equality

Who We Serve

01

First-Gen / Low-Income

Students from families earning below median income who will be first-generation college students. We believe in you, and we're here to level the playing field.

02

Physically Gifted

Athletes with demonstrable elite physical potential in their sport. Coaches see it. Scouts see it. You feel it. Now let's develop it.

03

Relentlessly Hungry

Athletes who understand that talent without discipline is potential wasted. You're willing to do whatever it takes—every single day.

You Are Who We're Looking For If:

You wake up thinking about your sport and go to sleep visualizing success

You're willing to sacrifice social time, screen time, and comfort for your goals

You see college athletics as a path to change your family's circumstances

You understand that elite performance requires elite discipline in all areas of life

You're coachable, humble, and hungry to learn from those who've succeeded

You want to be remembered not just as a great athlete, but as an exceptional person

What We Expect

→ Absolute adherence to sleep, nutrition, and recovery protocols

→ Maintaining a minimum 3.0 GPA with regular academic check-ins

→ Treating training and competition with professional-level discipline

→ Demonstrating character, sportsmanship, and leadership on and off the field

→ Regular progress reports and honest communication with coaches and mentors

→ Commitment to giving back and mentoring future Chancelete athletes

If you can't commit to all of this, we're not the right fit. If you can—let's go.
